Story Line (Java)

Characters: Gus the Gas Can (greenhouse gas), Sarah the Sun, Sid the Solar Panel

Story:

Gus the Gas Can kidnaps Sarah the Sun, so Sid the Solar Panel must rescue her.

Type of Game:

Side Scroller (ex. Mario, Sonic, Jetpack Joyrides)
Each level has a question at the end. The player must answer question to progress. They collect light bulbs, avoid coals/
oil drums, and other NPC's. Lights bulbs are used to buy hints to get past a                                                 level.
